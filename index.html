FIELD NOTE 01

HOW TO READ THE CITY

01

Observe a Step

Choose an Agent or a card to inspect their state, place and relationship trajectory.

02

Follow Time

Press play for a continuous time window. Dialogue finishes first; the final segment shows movement into the next observation.

03

Read Dialogues

Speech markers on the timeline jump to Steps with playable encounters. Dialogue mode rotates up to three encounters at once.

04

Trace Movement

FLOW controls Normal movement, ghosts or light trails. ROADS routes inter-venue movement along the street network.

05

Edit the Scene

Map Editor manages physical space; Character Generator assigns bundled pixel sprites. Neither changes the simulation record.

DATA BOUNDARY

Displayed positions and motion are visual layout aids. Replay coordinates, dialogue and relationship values remain the source evidence.
